WELCOME TO APPLEFEST 2017

Come join one of Ithaca's greatest events of the year, the Apple Harvest Festival! The Apple Harvest Festival more commonly known as 'Applefest' is an exciting weekend filled with various activities, performances, merchandise, foods, and more. Applefest is hugely famous for its selection of the freshest apples picked from this year's harvest, its mouthwatering delicacies made from the best of local produce, its goods that display the best craftmanship one only dreams of seeing, and its exhilarating sights from the best performers in the Tompkins County Area! So what are you waiting for? Plan your weekend to enjoy the best that Ithaca can offer!
