Hi, welcome to my site.

I am Ji Hwan Seung, a current junior undergraduate at Cornell University. I am majoring in Information Science with concentrations in data science and networks & markets. I love to cook, workout, and dance during my free time.

A lonely tree
Source: Taken by me in California

I come from an international background. I have lived in California for two years, Japan for four, and Connecticut for four. I was born in Korea, but most of my education was done abroad. Living in different places has allowed me to meet people from many different backgrounds.

The past five years have been life changing. I was accepted into Cornell in the spring of 2013, and first came to campus in the fall of the same year. After a long year of difficulties and hardships, I took a leave of absence to serve my conscription duty back in South Korea. After completing my military service by the fall of 2016, I travelled Europe, visiting France, England, Spain, Portugal, and Morocco, experiencing cultures and building relationships with new people. I learned to play golf, I made working out a habit, and I began to cook for myself. Five years is a long time, and the past five years have transformed who I am.
