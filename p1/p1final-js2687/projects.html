I am a developer. I love to build projects. This is one of the first practical projects that I built during one of my classes my sophomore year. This is a mobile weather application that fetches real-time weather data and displays it. One feature that I always wanted in a weather application was a simple way to compare today's weather with yesterday's, or tomorrow's weather with today's. Often times, reading just numbers in celsius or fahrenheit does not tell you much.

This is another application that I built over the summer. This is a very basic real-time chatting application that uses Google's Firebase console. This application, although very simple, taught me a lot about making network requests and learning how to manage databases at a simple level. It also helped me gain a stronger understanding of laying out content and other Swift fundamentals.
