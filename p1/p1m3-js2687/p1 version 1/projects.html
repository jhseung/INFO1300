I am a developer. I love to build projects. This is one of first practical projects that I built during one of my classes my sophomore year. This is a mobile weather application that fetches real-time weather data and displays it. One feature that I always wanted in a weather application was a simple way to compare today's weather with yesterday's, or tomorrow's weather with today's. Often times, reading just numbers in celsius or fahrenheit does not tell you much.
